You Name It. We Label It

ComputerLabel Worldwide,

established in 1979, has evolved as an apparel branding specialist and is known to deliver high quality trims and accessories to clothiers and designers worldwide. Our high standards in yarn denier, design clarity, texture and color satisfy the smallest custom designers to top fashion brands and retailers.

With our state-of –the-art manufacturing facilities in Thailand and China and sales offices in over ten different countries, we are committed to effectively and efficiently serving the worldwide needs of our clients and exceeding their expectations in quality, price, service and speed.
CLW Global – Since 1979
Welcome note

Being in this business for over three decades, we at Computerlabel Worldwide Co., Ltd. have always strived to provide user friendly products and services, delivered on time, strictly as per the designs and specification of the customers at competitive prices.

CLW has become a BOI supported company and has been at the forefront of the global label manufacturing industry. We provide intelligent, creative and sustainable branding solutions that convey strong brand image and value to customers.
Our wide range of product offerings help meet all our customer’s requirements and we are committed to ethical business practices.
Our passion is to ensure that your label buying experience is convenient, enjoyable and rewarding. We look forward to earning your business!
